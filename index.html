B I O G R A P H Y

Hello! After going to school for graphic design, I spent a few years dabbling in the design world while also honing my skills as a barista (as many art students do). I found myself more and more drawn to the technical aspects of design and recently began persuing a career pivot into web development. I recently started a code bootcamp called Epicodus and I am excited to see where life takes me!
